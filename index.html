About Me

My name is Brendan Sikorjak and I am a huge fan of the New York Rangers. I am a first generation born American to immigrant parents from Slovakia and Czechia. Playing ice hockey and tennis are my hobbies and I was lucky enough to turn one of those sports into a profession. During the spring, summer, and fall I coach tennis to children and seniors and have a blast doing it! I am currently pursuing my career in computer science at a bootcamp provided by Rutgers University to build my portfolio.
